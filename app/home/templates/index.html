Floppy Dog

Flop joined our family in September 2015. He filled our hearts with joy from the moment he arrived. As the name-sake of this bbq business, we know it’s obvious he’s the center of attention most everyday. He was the runt of the Maltese litter, but it has never stopped him! Nicknames include (but not limited too): FloppyDog, LittleDog, Flopdog, TinyDog, LittleSnackDog, SnoringDog, LazyDog, SaltyDog, … you catch the drift… but he answers to any of them or just the sound of a rustling bag! He’s tasted and approved the BBQ!
